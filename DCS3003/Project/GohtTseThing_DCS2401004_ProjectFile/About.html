About Iceland

Iceland, a Nordic island in the North Atlantic, is famous for its stunning landscapes, including glaciers, volcanoes, geysers, and hot springs. Notable sites include Eyjafjallajökull volcano and Vatnajökull glacier. Wildlife like puffins, whales, and seals add to its allure.


The Golden Circle is a must-see route, featuring Þingvellir National Park, where tectonic plates meet, the geothermal Geysir area, and the stunning Gullfoss waterfall. For relaxation, the Blue Lagoon offers a rejuvenating experience in a lava field with mineral-rich waters. Iceland's northern regions also provide opportunities for glacier hiking, ice caving, and viewing the Northern lights in winter.
