Kai'sa, Daughter of the Void



Kai'Sa is a champion from League of Legends, known for her unique background as a survivor of the Void. She was trapped in this dangerous realm for years, during which she formed a symbiotic bond with a living Void creature, granting her extraordinary powers. This connection allows her to wield both Void energy and human resilience, making her a versatile marksman. In the game, she excels at dealing burst damage and can adapt her abilities through itemization, embodying a blend of agility and strength in the battlefield.


Skill



  1. Passive - Second Skin
    • Caustic Wounds: Kai'Sa's basic attacks stack plasma on enemies. After several stacks, the plasma explodes, dealing bonus magic damage based on the target's missing health..
    • Living Weapon: Kai'Sa's abilities evolve based on the bonus stats she gains from items, adapting to her build path.

  2. Q - Icathian Rain
    3.
    • Kai'Sa fires a volley of missiles that split across nearby enemies. If multiple missiles hit the same target, they take additional damage. This ability evolves with additional bonus attack damage (AD), increasing the number of missiles fired.

  3. W - Void Seeker
    4.
    • Kai'Sa fires a long-range energy bolt that passes through enemies, marking them with plasma and revealing them. This ability evolves with more bonus ability power (AP), increasing the damage and refunding cooldown upon hitting champions.

  4. E - Supercharge
    5.
    • Kai'Sa charges up, gaining increased movement speed. After charging, she rapidly fires her basic attacks. This ability evolves with more bonus attack speed (AS), allowing her to briefly become invisible while charging up.

  5. R - Killer Instinct
    6.
    • Kai'Sa dashes at extreme speed to a location near an enemy champion marked with plasma, gaining a shield upon arrival. This ability is key for repositioning or diving onto vulnerable targets in team fights.




Akali, the Rogue Assassin


Akali is a champion from League of Legends, known for her lethal agility and mastery of stealth. A former member of the Kinkou Order, she has forged her own path as a vigilante, determined to protect her homeland from those who threaten it. With her signature shurikens and smoke bombs, Akali excels in ambushing enemies and swiftly dispatching her targets. Her abilities allow her to blend into the shadows, striking with precision and evading danger, making her a formidable presence in any battle.


Skill



  1. Passive - Assassin's Mark
    2.
    • When Akali hits an enemy champion with one of her abilities, a ring forms around the target. If Akali exits the ring, she gains bonus movement speed and her next basic attack is empowered to deal bonus magic damage and restore energy.

  2. Q - Five Point Strike
    3.
    • Akali throws five kunai in a cone, dealing damage to enemies in front of her. If cast with nearly full energy, it also slows enemies. This ability is great for poking and wave clear.

  3. W - Twilight Shroud
    4.
    • Akali drops a smoke bomb, creating a shroud that expands over time. While inside the shroud, Akali becomes invisible and gains bonus movement speed. She can also move through the shroud freely, making her hard to target. Attacking or using abilities will briefly reveal her.

  4. E - Shuriken Flip
    5.
    • Akali flips backward, throwing a shuriken forward. The shuriken deals damage and marks the first enemy or smoke cloud hit. Recasting the ability lets Akali dash to the marked target, dealing additional damage. This is both a mobility and damage tool that allows Akali to engage and re-engage.

  5. R - Perfect Execution
    6.
    • Akali dashes in a direction, dealing damage to all enemies she passes through. She can recast this ability to dash again, dealing additional damage based on the missing health of the target. This ability is excellent for both engaging and finishing off low-health enemies.




Ahri, the Nine-Tailed Fox


Ahri is a champion in League of Legends, renowned for her enchanting charm and cunning. A vastaya with the spirit of a fox, she possesses powerful magical abilities and a deep connection to the essence of life. Driven by curiosity and a desire to understand humanity, Ahri uses her charm to mesmerize her enemies, allowing her to manipulate the battlefield. With her agile movements and devastating spells, she can quickly burst down foes while evading danger. Ahri embodies the balance between beauty and danger, captivating all who cross her path.


Skill



  1. Passive - Vastayan Grace
    • Essence Theft: When Ahri hits an enemy with her abilities, she gains stacks of Essence Theft. Upon reaching 9 stacks, her next ability heals her for a percentage of damage dealt.
    • Vastayan Grace: After hitting two abilities, Ahri briefly gains a burst of movement speed.

  2. Q - Orb of Deception
    3.
    • Ahri throws her orb forward and then pulls it back, dealing magic damage on the way out and true damage on the way back. This ability can be used to poke enemies and clear waves quickly.

  3. W - Fox-Fire
    4.
    • Ahri summons three spectral flames that orbit her and target nearby enemies. If Ahri moves near an enemy, the flames will prioritize enemy champions.

  4. E - Charm
    5.
    • Ahri blows a kiss that charms and damages the first enemy hit. While charmed, the target walks harmlessly towards Ahri and takes increased damage from all of her abilities for a short duration.

  5. R - Spirit Rush
    6.
    • Ahri dashes forward, dealing damage to nearby enemies. Spirit Rush can be cast up to three times in quick succession, making Ahri incredibly mobile and able to quickly reposition or chase down fleeing enemies.
